Go abroad to get your Bachelor's degree!

Take the next step in your educational journey with our exclusive top-up program. After graduating from your Level 4 & 5 (HND) in Business, Computer Science, or Law, spend a transformative year abroad at one of our accredited partner universities. In this intensive program, you'll delve into advanced coursework gaining practical and theoretical insights. This unique experience offers flexible learning methods, providing in-depth insights into your chosen field. Upon successful completion, you'll be awarded a Bachelor's degree from a prestigious partner university abroad.

The Top-Up Year, equivalent to the final year of a Bachelor's degree, serves as a specialized enhancement program for graduates of Level 4 & 5 (HND) in Business, IT, or Law. Whether you pursue further studies or enter the workforce, our top-up program opens doors to a world of global employment opportunities. Elevate your career with an internationally recognized qualification.

Why go for an Undergraduate Top-Up?

Tailored to give you a global perspective, the Top-Up Year guides you through a hands-on professional journey. The qualification earned is internationally recognized, enhancing your prospects worldwide. Gear up for an enriched educational experience that propels you toward a successful career trajectory!

Potential benefits of our Top-Up programmes:

  • Specialized Training: The Top-Up Program acts as a specialized training initiative, focusing on law and business to enhance your expertise.
  • Get your Bachelor's Degree: Complete this program to get your Bachelor's Degree and experience life abroad.
  • Internationally Recognized Qualification: Earn a globally recognized qualification, providing a robust foundation for your international career aspirations.
  • Pathway Abroad: One of the program's standout advantages is the opportunity to choose and complete your final year's top-up at a university of your preference abroad.

Undergraduate Top-Up is your first step towards building your future globally.
